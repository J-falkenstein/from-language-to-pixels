The backslash symbol (\) is a diagonal line that slopes from the top left to the bottom right. It is inclined at a 45-degree angle and is straight and uninterrupted. The line starts at the top left corner and ends at the bottom right corner. The length of the line is typically equal to the height of the symbol, giving it a balanced and symmetrical appearance. The backslash is a simple yet distinctive symbol that stands out due to its diagonal orientation.
