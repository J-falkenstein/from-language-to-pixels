A cup is a cylindrical-shaped object with a round base and an open top. It is commonly used for holding liquids. The cup has a handle attached to its side, usually aligned with the base of the cup. The handle is small and curved, allowing for a comfortable grip. The proportions of the cup are such that the height is taller than the width. The top rim of the cup is smooth and circular, while the base is flat. The cup can be made of different materials such as glass, ceramic, or plastic. 

To summarize, a cup is a cylindrical object with a round base, an open top, and a handle aligned with the base.
